About us

Philosophy

Savor the Essence
of Japan.

Gently aging in a quiet alley of Ginza for over thirty years.
Over these decades, we have refined the essence of true, traditional flavor.
We face each ingredient with sincerity, selecting the finest treasures of the day.
Vegetables carrying the scent of the earth, seafood touched by the sea breeze.
Without disturbing their natural breath, we honor each element,
shaping it into a single dish with the skills cultivated over many years.

Preserving the subtle beauty of the passing seasons.
Please savor a tranquil moment, and enjoy to your heart’s content.

Serenity & Warmth

A dignified atmosphere that makes you forget the city's bustle.
Featuring a counter made from centuries-old Japanese cypress
and delicate Kumiko woodwork crafted by artisans,
our space offers a peaceful escape for our guests.
